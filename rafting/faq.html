What is Provided

When rafting with us, we provide everything you need. From the rafts to the lifejackets. We provide a complimentary snack, lifevests, paddles, rafts, water shoes, and other excerserise based on your perfered trip. For instance for a week long get away we provide the food, sleeping bags and tents. All in all when you raft with us the only thing you have to bring is, you.

Safety

Your safety is one of our priorities! If there are any medical concerns such as physical handicaps or other disabilities we are more than happy to accommodate in any way we can. We also verify to have a first aid kit and AED near by on the trip. All guides will also be trained in first aid.

Bad Weather and Other Issues

If there happens to be problems on the trip such as bad weather or medical emergency we will have an emergency response plan. This plan will consist of getting to shore, assessing the issues, then act appropriately. If there happens to be bad weather we will make it back to shore and wait in a complimentary van. If bad weather persists appropriate arrangements will be made for refunds and other such accommodations. If there is a medical emergency the guide will give appropriate directions to resolve the emergency to the best that can be done.

Why White Water Rafting

White water rafting is a fun activity for all ages, though we recommend the age of 8. This is a simple activity that all can be apart of. White water rafting provides a thrilling adventure that will be remembered for a lifetime. It is a great way to enjoy nature and cool off in the blistering heat that summer brings. Rafting can also provide family bonding and strengthening experiences, by working together.

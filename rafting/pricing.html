Bundles and Specials

If you are celebrating an aniversery or birthday you would love our special occasions discounts. After all these occasions don't just come around every day. Our discounts start at $700 (per person) for aniverseries and $750 (per person) for birthdays. We also have bundles prepared just for those who want to come in large groups (over 25) starting at $800 (per person) and anyone under 14 goes free. This is assuming it is for a week long trip. for day trips, aniverseries go for $75 (per person) for birthdays it is $95 (per person) and those under the age of 14 go free.

Weeklong Getaways

For a week long adventure it costs $900 (per person) and a stand alone fee of $550 for all the rest of the supplies that we will provide such as tents, sleeping bags, food, and everything else in between.

Day Trips

For a simple day trip it only costs $95 (per adult) and $75 (per anyone under 18)
